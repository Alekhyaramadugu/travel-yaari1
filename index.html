Popular Places

Venice dazzles with its iconic St. Mark’s Basilica and the historic Doge’s Palace. The Grand Canal offers stunning views from a gondola ride, while the Rialto Bridge is perfect for exploring local markets. Don’t miss the colorful Burano Island and the artistic treasures of the Peggy Guggenheim Collection.

Classic Gondola Ride

Gondolas have been a symbol of Venice for centuries, dating back to the 11th century when they were the primary mode of transportation through the city’s intricate canal system.
